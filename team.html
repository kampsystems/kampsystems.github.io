Our Team

KAMP Systems is a group of senior Electronic Systems and Engineering Technologies students that specialize in electronics and communications. Our project is to create a rotating arm assembly for a Cosmic Dust Collector. We are working together with Stratos Manufacturing, a senior Manufacturing and Mechanical Engineering Technologies, who specializes in manufacturing and mechanical design. Together we will use our combined expertise to create a electro-mechanical solution that will meet the needs of our client.
