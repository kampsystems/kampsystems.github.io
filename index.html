Welcome

This website is where you can get updated information on the progress of KAMP Systems and their Rotating Arm Assembly in the design of the Cosmic Dust Collector. KAMP Systems has partnered with NASA and T STAR to pursue this project. KAMP Systems is pursuing to create a product that will be fit to travel to space and return with comic dust samples so that NASA scientists can further study their them. These studies could lead to new scientific breakthroughs of the vast unknown we call the universe.
